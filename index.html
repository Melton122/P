Introduction

Welcome to VarsityBuddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "App").

By using VarsityBuddy, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our App.

Quick Summary: We only collect data necessary to provide our study tools, leaderboard, and tutor marketplace features. We never sell your data to third parties. You have full control over your information and can delete your account at any time.

2

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and communication
  • Full name — displayed on your profile and leaderboard
  • Phone & WhatsApp number — optional, for tutor contact features
  • Profile image — optional, uploaded by you
  • School name & Grade level — for academic context and matching
  • Province & City — for location-based features like tutor finding

Study & Academic Data

To provide our core study tools, we collect:

  • Flashcards — questions, answers, subjects, difficulty levels
  • Study plans & goals — titles, descriptions, deadlines, completion status
  • Exam schedules & reminders — dates, subjects, priority levels
  • Study reminders & alarms — scheduled times, recurrence patterns, completion status
  • Study session data — start/end times, subjects studied, XP earned
  • Streak data — consecutive study days, longest streaks
  • Subject preferences — selected subjects for personalized content

Gamification & Social Data

  • XP & Level progression
  • Total points & achievements
  • Leaderboard rankings — global and weekly
  • Bio & personal description — optional, publicly visible on profile

Tutor Booking Data

If you use our tutor marketplace:

  • Tutor session bookings — dates, times, subjects, status
  • Tutor reviews & ratings you submit
  • Tutor inquiries & messages
  • Favorite tutors list
  • Session reports — if you report an issue

Device & Technical Information

  • Device type & OS — iOS/Android version
  • Push notification tokens — for study reminders and announcements
  • App usage analytics — feature usage, crash reports, performance data
  • IP address — for security and fraud prevention
🔒
Data Minimization

We only collect data that is necessary to provide our services. Optional fields are clearly marked, and you can use most features without providing optional information.

Data Storage & Security

Storage Location

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure. Data may be processed and stored in data centers located in various regions to ensure reliability and performance.

Security Measures

🔐 Encryption in Transit

  • All data uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Secure HTTPS connections only

🛡️ Encryption at Rest

  • Sensitive data encrypted in database
  • Encrypted backup storage

🔑 Row Level Security

  • You only access your own data
  • Explicit sharing for public features

🔏 Secure Authentication

  • OAuth & JWT token-based auth
  • Regular security audits

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: After 24 months of inactivity, we may delete or anonymize your data
  • Deleted accounts: Personal data removed within 30 days; encrypted backups may retain data for up to 90 days

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We Do Not Sell Your Data. We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Trusted Service Providers

We share data only with trusted providers who help us operate the App:

  • Supabase — Cloud database and authentication services
  • Expo / EAS — Push notification delivery and app infrastructure
  • Cloud storage providers — Profile image storage
  • Analytics providers — Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and data protection obligations.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required to comply with a legal obligation, court order, government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Children's Privacy

VarsityBuddy is designed for students, including those under 18. We take special precautions:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent
  • If you are under 13, a parent or guardian must review and agree to this Privacy Policy
  • Parents/guardians may contact us to review, update, or delete their child's information
  • We do not display precise location data publicly
  • Direct messaging between users is moderated
👨‍👩‍👧
Parental Concerns?

If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately and we will take prompt action.

Push Notifications

VarsityBuddy uses push notifications for:

  • Study reminders and alarms you configure
  • Exam deadline alerts
  • Tutor booking confirmations and session reminders
  • Important app updates and announcements

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or in-app preferences. Note that disabling notifications will prevent study reminders from alerting you.

Tutor Marketplace Privacy

For Students

When booking tutors:

  • Your name and contact details are shared with the tutor for session coordination
  • Your reviews and ratings are publicly visible (associated with your display name)
  • Tutors cannot see your study data, flashcards, or personal reminders

For Tutors

If you apply to be a tutor:

  • Your application data is reviewed by our admin team
  • Once approved, your tutor profile is visible to students
  • Students can see your name, photo, subjects, rates, and reviews
